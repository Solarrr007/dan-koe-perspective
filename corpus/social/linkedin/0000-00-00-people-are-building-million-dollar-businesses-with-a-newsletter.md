## Raw Post

This post is one of Dan's cleanest minimalist statements about the modern creator-business stack.

The visible official LinkedIn post compresses the stack into three assets:

- a newsletter
- a social media account
- the knowledge in your head

Dan then ties those assets to a personal-development ladder: self-education, self-reliance, and self-improvement create high-value individuals, and high-value individuals get paid for their contributions on the internet.

## Main Position

- modern internet businesses can start with very little infrastructure
- knowledge plus distribution can be enough to build substantial leverage
- the creator-business path rewards personal development and useful contribution

## Business Framing

- newsletter and social media are enough to start distribution
- knowledge can be treated as a monetizable asset
- self-development is positioned as economic leverage, not just lifestyle advice
