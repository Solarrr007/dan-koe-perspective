## Structured Body

This landing page shows the commercial wrapper around Dan Koe's purpose narrative. Where the long-form guide explains the philosophy, this page compresses that philosophy into a clearer promise: help the user move from confusion, drift, and externally defined success toward a more self-authored life and business.

The page is useful because it shows which parts of Dan's worldview he believes are strong enough to sell directly. It highlights that the marketable version of his philosophy is not just "find purpose." It is a combination of clarity, meaningful work, self-monetization, and a different relationship to modern work.

This makes the landing page an important bridge between worldview content and offer design. It shows how philosophical writing gets turned into a product promise without fully losing its identity layer.
