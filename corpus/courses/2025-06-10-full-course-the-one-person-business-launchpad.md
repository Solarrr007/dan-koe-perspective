## Structured Body

This course page shows how Dan packages one-person business as an entry-level transformation. The framing is not "become a startup founder." It is "build a simple leveraged business around your skills, content, and products without needing a traditional team."

The page is valuable because sales pages reveal the clearest version of what the creator believes the audience wants to become. Here, the transformation appears to revolve around clarity, profitable skill selection, audience building, digital products, and a more realistic path into solopreneurship.

From a skill-building perspective, this page matters because it translates Dan's worldview into a structured teaching ladder. It turns abstract ideas like leverage, point of view, and creator systems into a progression the user can actually follow.
